# ClearifyAI.Privacy.Policy
Privacy Policy
Last updated: 2 February 2026

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, which may include:

Email address (if you choose to create an account)

Skin preferences and onboarding responses

Photos you upload or capture for skin appearance analysis

We do not collect medical records or health data.

Camera Usage

The camera is used solely to capture images for visual skin appearance analysis.
Camera access is optional, and the app remains usable if access is denied.

Images are used to generate informational insights and are not used for medical diagnosis, treatment, or prevention of any condition.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

Generate AI-based skin appearance insights

Provide personalized skincare recommendations

Display progress metrics based on visual analysis

Improve app functionality and user experience

All insights are for informational and cosmetic purposes only.

Photo Data

Photos used for analysis are processed securely.
Photos are:

Used only to generate results for you

Not sold or shared with advertisers

Not used for facial recognition or identity verification

If photos are temporarily stored for processing, they are handled securely and deleted when no longer needed.

Data Storage & Security

We use industry-standard security measures, including:

Encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS)

Secure storage systems

Access controls to protect user information

While no system is 100% secure, we take reasonable steps to protect your data.

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services for:

Analytics

Infrastructure

AI processing

These providers process data only as necessary to deliver their services and are bound by applicable data protection requirements.

Subscriptions

Subscription purchases are processed securely through Apple’s App Store.
We do not store payment or billing information.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

Access your personal data

Request correction or deletion

Withdraw consent at any time

You may request data deletion through the app or by contacting support.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.
Upon deletion, your personal information and uploaded images will be permanently removed from our systems, except where retention is required by law.

Children’s Privacy

Clearify is not intended for children under 13.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
Material changes will be communicated within the app.

Contact Us

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us through the Help & Support section in the app.
